# Support & suggestions

The way to get in touch about Obsidian Valet is through **GitHub Issues**:

- **Open an issue:** [github.com/fgsanz/obsidian-valet/issues](https://github.com/fgsanz/obsidian-valet/issues)

You will need a (free) **GitHub account** to create or comment on an issue. If you don't have one yet, you can sign up at [github.com/signup](https://github.com/signup).

## What you can raise

Issues are welcome for anything, for example:

- **Bug reports** — something isn't working as expected. Include what you did, what you expected, and what actually happened (steps to reproduce help a lot).
- **Questions** — you're unsure how a feature works or how to achieve something.
- **Suggestions** — ideas for new features, improvements, or changes to existing behavior.

When it helps, add details like your operating system, the version of Obsidian Valet you're running (see [Releases](releases)), and screenshots.

## Thank you

Thank you for taking the time to try Obsidian Valet and to share your feedback. Every report, question, and suggestion helps make the tool better.
